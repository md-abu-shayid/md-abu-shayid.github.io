Welcome to My Website!

Hello! I’m Md. Abu Shayid, a Bangladesh Civil Service (BCS) cadre officer posted in a Goverment college as an Assistant Professor in Mathematics. In addition, I am the chairman of the Information and Communication Technology (ICT) department.

I am an Assistant Professor of Mathematics and currently serve as the Chairman of the Department of ICT at a government college in Bangladesh. With a diverse academic background spanning mathematics and information technology, I hold an MSc in IT from the University of Aberdeen, an MPhil in Mathematics with a research focus on fuzzy topological spaces, and both a BSc and MSc in Mathematics from the University of Rajshahi. My teaching encompasses mathematics and ICT, and I support student projects, particularly in programming (C, FORTRAN). My current research interests lie in Federated Learning and Brain Anomaly Detection from Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI), and I aim to pursue a PhD in this area.

I’m always eager to collaborate on innovative ideas and explore new opportunities. Please feel free to browse my website to learn more about my educational background, research, and professional journey. Thank you for stopping by!
